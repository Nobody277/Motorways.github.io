How to Use

  • Water Tool: Click and drag to create bodies of water (obstacles)
  • Road Tool: Click and drag to create roads for cars to travel on
  • House Tool: Place 2x3 houses that generate cars of the selected color
  • Building Tool: Place 4x3 commercial buildings that receive cars of matching color
  • Erase Tool: Click to remove roads, buildings or water

Cars will automatically find the shortest path from houses to matching-colored buildings.
